# Chonky Apple Sauce

> Best made with hand picked apples

## Ingredients

| Ingredient | Amount|
| :------ |:--- |
| Large Apples | 8 - 10 |
| Water | 1/2 Cup | 
| Cinnamon | 1 tsp |
| Sugar | 3/4 Cup|

## Instructions

Mix all ingredients in a crockpot.
Let sit on hight for 3 - 4 hours. 

> Serve cold over oatmeal for a delicious breakfast

Enjoy!
